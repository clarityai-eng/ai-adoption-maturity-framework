* Squad name / domain:  
* Roles present (FE, BE, Data, QA, EM, DS, MLE, etc.):  
* Seniority mix:  
* Main tech stack:  
* Type of work (product, platform, infra, data, etc.):

## **2\. General Perception of AI Coding (5 min)**

**Objective:** Identify baseline attitude and narratives.

**Questions**

* When you hear “AI-assisted coding”, what comes to mind?  
* How would you describe the squad’s overall attitude toward it?  
  * Very positive / cautiously positive / neutral / skeptical / negative  
* What do you see as the **main benefits**?  
* What are the **main concerns or drawbacks**?

**Signals to capture**

* Enthusiasm vs. fear  
* Quality, security, IP, dependency, productivity narratives

## **3\. Adoption Level & Usage Patterns (10 min)**

**Objective:** Understand *who uses it*, *how often*, and *for what*.

**Questions**

* Do you personally use AI coding tools?  
  * Yes / Occasionally / No  
* How often?  
  * Daily / Weekly / Ad hoc / Rarely  
* In which situations do you use them? (probe)  
  * Code generation  
  * Refactoring  
  * Debugging  
  * Test generation  
  * Documentation  
  * Learning unfamiliar code or tech  
* At what stage of work?  
  * Exploration / Implementation / Review / Fixing bugs

**Signals**

* Individual vs. collective usage  
* Narrow vs. broad use cases  
* Experimental vs. habitual usage

## **4\. Tools & Setup (5–10 min)**

**Objective:** Map tooling maturity and constraints.

**Questions**

* What tools are you using today?  
  * (e.g. Copilot, ChatGPT, Cursor, IntelliJ AI Assistant, other, none)  
* How are they accessed?  
  * IDE-integrated / browser ~~/ company-approved setup~~  
* Are there restrictions or policies that affect usage?  
* Have you customized prompts, workflows, or settings?

**Signals**

* Official vs. shadow usage  
* Tool sprawl  
* Depth of configuration vs. default usage

## **5\. Practices & Quality Control (10 min)**

**Objective:** Assess maturity beyond raw usage.

**Questions**

* How do you validate AI-generated code?  
* Are there agreed practices or rules of thumb?  
  * What is acceptable vs. not?  
* Has AI usage ever caused:  
  * Bugs?  
  * Security concerns?  
  * Maintainability issues?  
* Do you use AI in code reviews? How?

**Signals**

* Blind trust vs. critical use  
* Explicit safeguards  
* Integration with engineering standards

## **6\. Learning, Enablement & Knowledge Sharing (5–10 min)**

**Objective:** Understand scaling mechanisms.

**Questions**

* How did you learn to use these tools?  
  * Self-taught / peers / training  
* Does the squad share prompts, tips, or workflows?  
* Have you run demos, brown bags, or experiments together?  
* What would help you use AI coding more effectively?

**Signals**

* Organic vs. structured learning  
* Knowledge silos  
* Appetite for enablement

## **7\. Impact & Outcomes (5 min)**

**Objective:** Tie usage to value.

**Questions**

* Has AI coding changed your productivity? How?  
* Has it changed code quality, speed, or confidence?  
* Where has it clearly *not* helped?  
* If AI tools disappeared tomorrow, what would you miss most?

**Signals**

* Perceived vs. measurable impact  
* Over- or under-estimation of value

## **8\. Maturity Self-Assessment (5 min)**

**Objective:** Quick calibration.

Ask the squad to self-rate (1–5):

* AI coding adoption  
* Skill level using tools effectively  
* Integration into daily workflows  
* Shared best practices  
* Confidence in quality & safety

Follow-up:

* Why this score?  
* What would move you one level up?

## **9\. Open Feedback & Closing (5 min / async)**

**Objective:** Capture insights not covered.

* What should the company **encourage more**?  
* What should it **limit or clarify**?  
* Any tools or practices we should explore or avoid?

## **Pioneers, Followers, and Detractors**

**Objective:** Identify change agents and blockers.

**Questions**

* Are there clear **power users / pioneers** in the squad?  
  * Who are they? What do they do differently?  
* Are there people who explicitly avoid or resist using AI tools?  
  * Why?  
* How do these differences play out in day-to-day work?  
* Do pioneers influence others? How?

**Signals**

* Informal leadership  
* Peer learning  
* Silent resistance vs. vocal skepticism
